STORYTELLING AND
CHARACTER DESIGN

Adobe illustrator

Introduction

What creates a good story? And why is it so important to be able to use stories? When I was working on this task I did not feel that I am creating a story, but connection. I believe that creating connection with people is crutial point of success.

Just with the connection we are able to be heard not just with senses but also with the emotions.

Task

Create a love or horror story. The dramaturgic curve has to consist of a prelude, point of no return, climax and a fade out. Define your message. Define the genre/mood. Draw a narrative curve of your story. Plan your use of dramaturgic elements.

Approach

This task contained a lot of small tasks. That is why, it was very important to finish every small task on the deadline. Youtube tutorials and advises from the teachers helped me to learn to draw on Adobe Illustrator. Following dramaturg curve helped me to have more impactful story.

Challanges

Create a story which is understandable and interesting for audience. Have a clear point of no return.

Tools

Adobe illustrator, Photoshop.

Purpose

Sketch and draw graphics in Illustrator ,drawing of characters for use in multimedia productions, Drawing graphics with a consistent visual expression in a specific style.
