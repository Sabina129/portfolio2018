Video group work

Introduction

In this group work assignment we also created the video where no words are spoken. This task was about to create the video for theme City walk where we show the walking in the city.

It was up to us how we are going to show it and so we tried to be creative with our idea of the story from the perspective of shoes of two different people.

My role

Editing the video, editing and choosing the sound of the video, part of the idea developmnet.

Group Members
  • Joao Camarao
  • Lana Sladovic
  • Nicoleta Smilkova
  • Rihard Zeila
  • Rolandas Eitutis

Task

Target audience are people that are not from Denmark, but have moved to and are living in Copenhagen. The video must be embedded on a one-page campaign website.

Approach

We had 3 group meetings where we all tried to form one idea, which is possible to make with our skills and time. We splitted our individual work based on the strengths, but also based on the opportunities for those who wanted to strengthen their weaknesess.

Challenges

Editing, idea, which is possible to make with our skills and time.

Tools

Brackets, Premiere Pro, Adobe Audition

Purpose

Create a video that introduces the concept for the target audience for city.

Behind
Video

We lost a bit of our time, because we changed our ideas often. Eventually we agreed on the story about the woman and the man from the shoes perspective . We wanted to highlight two things. Their personalities and differences. For this purpose we had the idea of creating the video as double screen, which will show what are the main characters doing in the same time. The woman is highlighted as a more social and the man as loner. We decided to use this difference for the target group which is new in the city and so many times they are feeling lonely, searching for someone who can introduce them for social life.

Point of no return is in the scene where the two characters meet each other and the screen becomes one. After they are walking together, discovering Copenhagen and have a good time. The goal and the message with the video is : walking together is better than alone.

What we could done better:

We could make the story shorter
The point of no return could be more clear.
We could film more movie shots from the city.
