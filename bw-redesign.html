Group work

Introduction

This project is the first group project I had within the field of Multimedia Design and Communication. Every group got the website which is going against design principles.

With the knowledge we gained within the first weeks at school, our main goal is to divide the tasks in order to redesign the website.

My role

Conducting user testing, reducing content, creating graphics and XD prototypes for the redesign website.

Group Members
  • Damir Dzelilovic
  • Larisa Ailisoaie
  • Ovidiu Constantin
  • Mihael Sandro

Task

Analyse the website with the purpose of making an improved redesign. Create a new visual design (colour scheme, typography, images). Creating an improved, more user-friendly, and responsive redesigned version of a website.

Approach

My approach was to constantly work on the project few hours a day in order to prevent stress from deadline. As a group we always tried to agree on every decision we made together. During the project I learnt, that the group can not be always satisfied with every decision. More important is to know when to compromise and trust the idea from a group member.

Challenges

No one from the group had enough skills in coding.

Tools

Adobe XD, Trello for organizing the work in group, collaboration agreement.

Purpose

Learn about working in groups and managing the project of making a basic redesign of an existing website as well as testing and presenting the final results in class.

Behind
Design

We spent a lot of time analysing Gates N Fences website in order to come up with the most effective way to make this website more user friendly. Firstly, we defined the problems.

After the analysis, we had to define the framework of redesign. We created many different layouts for this task to determine the best functionality.

Inspiration

It was time to begin to redesign the fonts and the colors.

We were analyzing which types of colors are using the competitors and modern websites. We tried to match.

We observed that in most cases, the websites used mainly white, grey and black colors where contrast create the pictures. The fonts were also minimalistics, mainly thin.

After we decided that black, white and grey look with contrast of pictures and thin font can be for user friendly and familiar, we started to create our mood of the website.

Logo & Prototypes

We wanted that the name of the company is very visible, because it does not show just the name of the company but what they actually sell. Then it is very easy for the user to instantly figured it out about what is the site about. Black and grey colors are used for representing the dominant colors of the site to keep consistence.

We wanted to choose font for the logo which will remind us the shapes of gates and fences. The font: Corvinus skyline icg

We did no have time to finish the whole prototype, because we had a lot of coding challenges to take care of. Luckily, we managed to conduct user tests before and after which help us to keep us on track. Keeping design principles helped us to succesfully redesign the Gates N Fences website.
