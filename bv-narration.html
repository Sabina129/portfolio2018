Without words

Video group work

Introduction

Is it possible to tell a story without speaking? This assignement is the example, that we are not speaking just with the sounds of our voices.

Our task was to create as a group the video about busy life, where we can’t use any spoken word. Let’s see how we handled it.

My role

Editing the video, editing and choosing the sound of the video, part of the idea developmnet, part of the video content creation.

Group Members
  • Joao Camarao

  • Nicoleta Smilkova
  • Rihard Zeila
  • Sergiu Gheorghe

Task

Tell a story in images alone. The story is telling about busy live. The video should be no longer than 2 minutes.

Approach

We had one group meeting where we all presented our ideas, agreed on one and then created the scenes and video. We tried to make it as simple as possible.

Challenges

No words can be spoken in the video

Tools

Brackets, Premiere Pro, Adobe Audition

Purpose

Using creative thinking, learning to create video with the low budget.

Behind
Video

We had 3 days for this assignment. The obstacle was that this group was formed later, because some of the group members were not present.
To be honest we had many great ideas, but they would required more time to be efficient. We needed to be creative within a one day. Our creative thinking led us to our personal experiences of busy life, which we can feel every day.

Messenger notifications are distracting us daily through out the day with many messages about a duties, advertisments, friends calls and list goes on. We perceive it as a normal thing, because we live in this age of technology since we can remember. But is it really normal? Can we always handle it without stress? We as a group believe, that we can show how can our hidden busy life without spoken words looks like.

Our steps were simple. We decided about the idea of making video through “mobile” perspective, where the main character Paul is writing on messanger.

Then we created roles for ourselves. The roles were - Paul’s girlfriend, boss at work and classmate from school.
We decided that we will create the script, where all of us will rush Paul to do something with impossible time limits. He will try to handle it.

After we created the script we were recording the screen. We started to write messeges according to the roles we had.
The video was succesfully made after 3 attemps. The last process was to add some more footages of Paul as an opening scene and ending scene for the audience to see him as a main character with his phone.
Then we could edit the video and add the emotional music. We chose the suspense music intentionaly in order to add our story more tension.
