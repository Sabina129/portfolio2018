About Me

My name is. Wait. It is about me and I don’t like to start so commonly. What is so typical for me is, that I like to search under the surface. Whatever it is me, you, my actions and design, I like to look deep. That is why this site is called yourdeepdesign. I desire that you will find in my projects always more than you expected.

I don't want to look on my design like design. I want to bring an observer of my design into my emotions, experience and everything what you can see just under the surface.

For my future clients I desire to create a design which will fully express what they want. I believe that just with the thoughtful deeper design we can connect with our customers in another level.

The deeper design=the deeper connection. But what do I mean by deep. It is more true to ourselves expression of what we are doing and offer. No more "we want to do something because we like it” no more "I want to offer this product, because I believe that people need it”. We need more WHY behind our actions and have an eye for a small details which lays under.

Now you gained a glimpse of what do I believe in and where my values are set for a design. But who is behind these words? My name is Sabina Sandorova. I am 21 years old currently studying Multimedia Design and Communication in Denmark. But am I danish? Nope. I am from less developed country called Slovakia. I believe that my country learned me to be grateful but don't settle for less. And so, now I left my country to gain even more knowledge and put them into more practise. I like to contribute with my presence into better living in our lives. That is pretty much it! And the lesson which I learned with my first design was. Compare just with ourselves not with others.
